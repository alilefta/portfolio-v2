Here is how to do text gradients correctly in Tailwind so they handle multiline wrapping.

```tsx
<h1 className="bg-linear-to-r from-blue-500 to-purple-500 box-decoration-clone bg-clip-text text-transparent">
  Hello World
</h1>
```
